About Us

Black-led organisations are agile, responsive and embedded in the communities they serve. Their considerable social capital and anecdotal data make them perfect partners for the public sector whose services they are frequently supplementing through their culturally focused innovating often delivered through large donations of resources and unpaid hours.


Griot was established as a research and development charity to promote African-centred approaches to developing people and communities to meet unmet community development needs. Our initial research into the governance and sustainability skill needs of African and Caribbean Trustees resulted in the co-creation of a long programme of dinner and discussion forums on a wide range of training, collaboration and partnership topics.


Out of these programmes came a specific public health programme for women with chronic fibroids to connect women seeking information with clinical, medical and complementary therapists, expert in the management and treatment of uterine fibroids.
