About Us

Griot was registered as a charity in 1998 and has been run mostly on volunteering and occasionally with very small grants. Training through discussion forums, seminars and workshops; infrastructure development and fundraising support for new groups and small businesses; action research. Health promotion through expert-led forums by clinical, medical, naturopathic and psycho-therapeutic professionals with proven expertise in the management of fibroids in women of African heritage; advocating NHS changes to meet needs.
